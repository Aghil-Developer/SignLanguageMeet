Platform Features

Explore the complete set of features that make MOUNA a powerful communication tool for accessibility.

📹

Peer-to-Peer Video Calls

Create or join rooms instantly. WebRTC-powered calls ensure low latency and high quality video without intermediary servers.

🎤

Real-Time Speech Recognition

The Web Speech API listens to conversations and transcribes them live. Works directly in the browser with no plugins.

✋

Sign Language Video Playback

When recognized words match our sign language dictionary, corresponding videos play in a floating overlay panel.

📄

Multi-Language Subtitles

View live subtitles in English, Telugu, Tamil, or Malayalam. Switch languages on the fly during a call.

🔇

Microphone Toggle

Mute and unmute your microphone with one click. Visual indicators show your current audio state.

📷

Camera Toggle

Turn your camera on and off as needed. Your privacy, your control.

🔗

Room System

Simple room-based system – create a room and share the ID, or join with an existing room ID. No accounts needed.

🎨

Dual Theme Support

Choose between the Vibranium Purple theme inspired by Black Panther, or the clean MongoDB Green theme.

📱

Responsive Design

Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices. Adaptive layout ensures a great experience everywhere.
